Justice League

The Justice League is a team of fictional superheroes appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. The Justice League was conceived by writer Gardner Fox, and they first appeared together in The Brave and the Bold #28 (March 1960).The Justice League is an assemblage of superheroes who join together as a team.

The six original members were:

  1. Aquaman
  2. Batman
  3. Flash
  4. Green Lantern
  5. Superman
  6. Wonder Woman
The team roster has rotated throughout the years, consisting of many superheroes from the DC Universe, such as
  • Atom
  • Black Canary
  • Cyborg
  • Green Arrow
  • Green Lantern (John Stewart)
  • Elongated Man
  • Hawkgirl
  • Hawkman
  • Plastic Man
  • Orion
  • Red Tornado
  • Stargirl
  • Captain Marvel/Shazam
  • Zatanna
  • and among others
